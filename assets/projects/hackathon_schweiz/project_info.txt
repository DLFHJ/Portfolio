Description: During the START Hack in St. Gallen, our team took on a challenge for the United Nations G20 Global Land Initiative. We focused on the Sahel region in Africa, where desertification makes land management incredibly difficult.

Our solution was a dashboard that simplifies years of complex satellite data into clear, actionable guidance for local decision-makers. As the UX designer, I focused on the overall concept and making sure the data was actually easy to understand and use. I also led our final presentation, where we successfully pitched a working prototype that we built from scratch during the hackathon.
