Short Description:
Description: Developed as a conceptual study during my Master’s in UX Design, this project challenged us to envision a sustainable smartphone ecosystem—spanning UI/UX, marketing strategy, and physical touchpoints. I led the general concept development and headed the packaging experience. My goal was to translate our core brand philosophy into a tactile reality, using Blender to create high-fidelity 3D renders that highlight a minimalist, eco-conscious aesthetic.
