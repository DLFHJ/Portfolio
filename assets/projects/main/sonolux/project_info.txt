Project Name: Sanolux
ID: 3
Category: Visual Concept & Design
Status: Completed
Key Dates: 2024
Technologies: Adobe Suite, Touchdesigner
Important Notes: 
Description: Sonolux is an immersive audio-visual installation showcased at Klanglicht (Graz) and the LUNA Festival (Eindhoven). As part of a five-person team, I collaborated with sound designers to explore how technology shapes our environment, using the Hilmteich in Graz as our conceptual subject.

My role focused on the visual architecture and generative design. Using TouchDesigner, I developed audio-reactive visuals based on my own photography and film of the site. These visuals morphed in real-time to reflect three distinct sonic states: the present, a utopia, and a dystopia. For the physical installation, we projected onto moving fabric animated by fans—creating a kinetic projection mapping experience that turned over four hours of field recordings into a dynamic, atmospheric spectacle.

-

Situated within the historic Grazer Schlossberg, the installation benefited from a truly unique atmosphere, allowing the visuals to interact with the cavernous, one-of-a-kind location.

-

Following its debut in Graz, the project was also selected for the LUNA Festival in Eindhoven, allowing us to adapt the installation for an international audience and a new architectural context.
