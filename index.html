About Me

I specialize in Interaction Design and bridging the gap between design and technology.

Fluent in both design and technology, I bridge the gap between creative vision and technical execution. I immerse myself deeply in the user’s perspective to transform complex systems into intuitive experiences. Whether building large-scale installations or mobile apps, my goal is to advocate for the user and create technology that feels seamless, purposeful, and human.
